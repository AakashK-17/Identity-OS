/ how it works

Four moves from JD to interview-ready PDF.

Build your foundation once. Paste a job description. Hone reads the role, sharpens the resume, and files the full application artifact into your history.

01 / FOUNDATION

Build your base profile.

Identity, experience, projects, skills, education, and certifications are stored once under your account.

02 / SIGNAL

Paste any job description.

The system extracts role, company, tools, hidden hiring signals, and seniority cues.

Tailor the resume.

Summary, bullets, projects, and core competencies are rewritten without inventing facts.

04 / MEMORY

Download and track.

DOCX, PDF, JD, company, role, and timestamp are saved into searchable history.
